Fourteen Angles
a collection of haiku
about life, the universe and everything
by Hans Lachman, 2022

Dinner with Friends

This will save the world:
In-person dinner with friends,
Sometimes friends of friends.

My Bio 2022

A colorful past.
A creative neurotic.
Now, just a recluse.

My Philosophy of Philosophy

Each philosophy,
Needed by some but not all,
Like a prescription.

My Religion

G.O.D. stands for:
Gratitude, Optimism,
Decency. That's all.

Comparative Religion

Many religions.
If it gets you there, it's right.
Like a vehicle.

My Planet

Earth has a purpose.
It's the insane asylum
Of the universe.

Happiness and Unhappiness

Happy, unhappy.
Both of them, useful signals.
Lights on a dashboard.

A How-to Guide

To be happy, ask:
What are your needs and your dreams?
Move forward on that.

Improvement to Maslow

Hierarchy of needs:
Resources, relations, and
Relevance. Three R's!

More R's

Recreation, and
Rest and relaxation too.
Maslow forgot these.

A Nod to Osho-Rajneesh

Zorba the Buddha.
Inner journey, outer life.
Two wings of a bird.

What is Tantra

Inside and outside:
Awareness, acceptance, and
Appreciation.

The Divine Play

In the play of life,
God is a method actor.
Do you think you're you?

What to Do with People

Life has transactions.
Balance with co-creation
And companionship.
